Biography

Slut Machine is an all-girls band with a satirical name, founded in September 2011 in Italy. The four girls immediately dedicated themselves to compose original pieces. The original sound was rough and scratchy, similar to bands such as G.B.H., The Exploited, the first Metallica... but now it has developed to include more rhythm and melodic vocals.

The line-up is composed by Melian (vocals), Camiska (vocals), Spring (bass & backing vocals), Xina (drums & backing vocals) and Sarah (guitars).

In September 2014 Slut Machine will release its first full-length (following the 2012 demo Hang-on) called Splatter Island, a concept album with 11 tracks that reveals the new sound of the band.

Slut Machine had the opportunity to play on special occasions:

  • February 2012 at A.W.O.D. Festival in London, with Antipasti and Sub Humans
  • August 2013 at Complete Control Festival (Poviglio, Italy), on the same stage of Agnostic Front
  • March 2014 in the south of France, two night shows with some thrash-hardcore local bands
  • ...and now they will return in France and travel to Germany to present the new album!
