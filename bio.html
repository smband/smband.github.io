Biography

Slut Machine is an all-girls band with a satirical name, founded in September 2011 in Italy. The girls immediately dedicated themselves to compose original pieces. The original sound was rough and scratchy, similar to bands such as G.B.H., The Exploited, the first Metallica... but now it has developed to include more rhythm and melodic vocals.

The most recent line-up is a power trio composed by Xina (drums & lead vocals), Spring (bass & backing vocals), and Sarah (guitars).

In September 2014 Slut Machine released their first full-length (following the 2012 demo Hang-on) called Splatter Island, a concept album with 11 tracks that reveals the new sound of the band.

Slut Machine had the opportunity to play on special occasions:

  • February 2012 at A.W.O.D. Festival in London, with Antipasti and Sub Humans
  • August 2013 at Complete Control Festival (Poviglio, Italy), on the same stage of Agnostic Front
  • March 2014 in the south of France, two night shows with some thrash-hardcore local bands
  • September 2014, a little tour in Germany
  • November 2015 and April 2016 in the south of France, two night shows with some thrash-hardcore local bands and U-Turn
