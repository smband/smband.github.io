BIOGRAPHY

Slut Machine is an Alt-Rock female power trio from Italy.

The band released an EP "Hang On" (2012), the first album "Splatter Island" (2014) and the latest album "Irrational" in 2017.

The band has toured all over Italy, France, the U.K. and Germany also sharing the stage with some big names such as Subhumans, Antipasti, Raw Power and Agnostic Front.
Slut Machine was formed in 2011 by Federica Rossi (Xina, Drums) and Primavera Tuci (Spring, Bass) and has seen many different line-up changes until the arrival of Sara Sivilotti (Sara) on guitar in 2016, right on time to write and record "Irrational".

Slut Machine is now ready to kick some ass and tour around the world for the promotion of the new album "Irrational"!

IRRATIONAL is the 3rd album from the Italian female power trio Slut Machine.
The album title represents the variety of musical influences throughout the 11 tracks but not only that. IRRATIONAL also means writing music without mental schemes or boundaries. The 11 songs on this record are an uncontrolled outburst and free expression of what the 3 musicians have to say.
